## Minimum Requirements


#### Homework


You must complete **90%** of the homework assignments. (You can miss **no more** than **2 assignments**.)


Homework submissions **must be on time AS IS**. Late submissions will not be counted.


#### Attendance


Attendance must be maintained at a **95%** rate. (You can miss **no more** than a total of **4 classes**.)


If you cannot be in-person for a particular class, you can avoid it being logged as one of your 4 absences by doing the following:
 - obtain written permission to miss the class (direct message your instructor on slack).
 - watch the class video (either streaming live or somteime later)
 - write a paragraph explaining what you learned from the class and submit it to your instructor


#### Projects


You must give a full effort on every group and individual project.
